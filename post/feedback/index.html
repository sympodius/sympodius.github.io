Feedback

Tue, Feb 12, 2013

Thank you so much to all of you for reading my stories! It is greatly encouraging to know that people are reading the things I write.

People have been particularly kind in their praise of my ideas. That is really wonderful to hear and makes me think this is all worthwhile. I am writing these short stories so that I can improve, and for that I need your help.

It’s so encouraging to see the hit count increase when I publish a new chapter, but I also value direct feedback. A few people have already been kind enough to take the time to give me thoughts and advice on my writing (and the site in general) and I greatly appreciate it. If you felt shy or awkward about giving me feedback, please ignore that feeling. Feedback is what this site is all about. Feedback will help me get better.

Just so we’re totally clear:

I REALLY WANT YOUR FEEDBACK.

Lastly, a little pre-apology: If I don’t agree with your feedback or don’t make the changes you suggest, please don’t think I don’t appreciate you taking the time. It’s not personal. You may be right and I may be wrong, but sometimes being wrong is something I need to go through before I’ll learn from it. Even if I don’t always agree with your suggestions, I still value knowing what you are thinking and that you took the time to tell me.

Thanks again for all your support and especially to those who have already given me their own critique. If you want to join the party, you can contact me through keybase.

Hope to hear from you soon!
